Catering

We use only the best quality meats, cheeses and products. All meat and cheese is sliced fresh on the day of pick-up. We guarantee you won’t be disappointed!

Pricing

Meat and Cheese Platters: Include meats and cheeses of your choice, loaves of bread (white, wheat, or rye), plates, napkins, utensils and condiments (mayo, mustard, horseradish, etc.).

Rather have the sandwiches already made for you? No problem! We are happy to make trays of sandwiches. You can count on us to make a beautiful presentation.

Call for pricing.

On-Site Catering

If you have an event requiring on-site catering, we can cater your event as well. We have grilled burgers, hotdogs, metts and brats on site for company picnics, as well as a wedding reception.

We serve our famous burgers, with sides of cheeses, onion, pickle, lettuce, tomato. Soups are also available by the quart. Available soups are chili, mock turtle, bean and ham, and vegetable beef.

We typically need 48 hours notice for larger orders. Please place your order with the location you would like to pick your order up from:

Mason: 513-229-0222, Norwood: 513-731-4370.
