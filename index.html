Our Story

Quatman Cafe was founded in 1966 by Albert (Albo) Imm and Ken Talmage. Quatman Cafe has become a Cincinnati Icon know for cheeseburgers, chili, homemade soups, and the coldest beer in town.

We've won several awards for Best Cheeseburger in Cincinnati and have been featured in Cincinnati Magazine, The Cincinnati Enquirer, CityBeat, aol.com, and many other publications.

Quatman Cafe is a friendly family-oriented restaurant. We offer a deli with local and national brands. Catering is also available.

We remain a family owned and operated restaurant commited to providing quality food in great locations at an affordable price.
